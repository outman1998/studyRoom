About studyRoom

Studyroom is a platform on which courses in a number of subjects are provided by tutors. Our application helps students, no matter where they may be, face their challenges with various subjects. The end goal is to improve the access to quality education everywhere and in the end create more opportunities to get decent work. Please visit our about page to learn more.

About courses

The courses on our platform are created by willing tutors. The system in place allows teachers all around the world set up courses for those in need. In turn, our students gain FREE access to these courses to help overcome their educational struggles. Simply sign up and visit the courses section to get started!
